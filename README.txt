# Corporate Asset Tracking App

The Corporate Asset Tracking App is a Django application designed to help companies track and manage their corporate assets, such as phones, tablets, laptops, and other devices, that are assigned to employees for a certain period of time.

## Features

- Companies can add and manage their employees.
- Employees can be assigned one or more devices.
- Devices can be checked out and returned, with a log of the assigned employee and the condition of the device.
- Companies can view the history of when a device was checked out and returned.
